Set Up Notifications

Every weekday at 10:00am, a notification is broadcast via ntfy to remind users to log their office presence.

To receive notifications please follow these steps:

Mobile Notifications

  1. Download ntfy app by following the instructions on their website: https://ntfy.sh/
  2. Tap the '+' button in the top right corner to add a subscription.
  3. Enter xbd_au under topic name.

Desktop Notifications

  1. Navigate to the ntfy web app: https://ntfy.sh/app. (Grant notification permissions if not already granted).
  2. Click 'Subscribe to topic'
  3. Enter xbd_au under topic name.
