1. Information We Collect

1.1. Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we store:

  • A unique account identifier
  • Associated display name or username

1.2. Attendance Data

  • Attendance records (date and attendance state)
  • User-provided notes

1.3. Usage Data

We maintain logs of service usage and performance for operational purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Calculate attendance statistics
  • Monitor and improve Service performance
  • Ensure security of the Service

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1. We store only essential data required for Service functionality.

3.2. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Auth0 to provide authentication services. When you sign in with a social login (e.g., GitHub), the provider supplies Auth0 with basic profile information, and Auth0 supplies us an identifier and display name to create and manage your account. Auth0 and the social login provider process information under their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Receive an export of your data

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of any material changes through the Service.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact contact@officetracker.com.au
